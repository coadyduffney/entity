About Us

We raid on an extremely light schedule (2 days, 6 hrs/wk) and expect the very best from our raiders. Our raiders are both mechanically sound and skillful within their respective class. Our goal is to down bosses in a timely manner while having fun as a group. With that being said, new recruits can expect a player base with a similar mindset. We are not a group of elitists and toxicity towards each other is unacceptable. As a new raider to the guild, you can expect a positive raiding environment with constructive criticism from our raiders.

This isn't just a guild of people raiding together online; it's a guild built on friendships made while enjoying a game we all play. With that said, our goals are the same as before - achieve good progression ranks and enjoy playing the game with solid raiders on a more laid-back schedule.

We raid Wednesday and Thursday from 7pm to 10pm EST. Raid invites go out ten minutes before raid. Players should be online and ready to zone in and start clearing trash at this time. This means you should have your coins for bonus loot, flasks, potions, food, spec, and knowledge of the encounters we'll be facing that night. We also have an optional raid to clear other content on Tuesday from 7pm to 10pm. While it is optional, we use this time to gather gear for those in need.

Expectations

  • Know your class inside-out and showcase that knowledge via performance. Study, understand, and analyze boss encounters. We seek players who quickly grasp the bigger picture of an encounter rather than just memorize the necessary steps to succeed.
  • Go above and beyond. Perform in situations where others are counting on you to do your job while maintain a can-do attitude in the face of criticism and wipes. When you make a mistake and it's brought to your attention, we expect you to accept it and move on, but more importantly, fix it.
  • Have a good sense a humor. A microphone is required to join, so you might as well use it once in a while. We aren't looking for people who raid log without saying a single word to us. This is a community, and you need to be a part of it if you wish to stick around. You will also need to respond to criticism in a positive manner and lashing out to another raider for any reason at all is unacceptable.
  • Attendance. Maintain at least a 90% raid attendance. Not only does your attendance play an important role with your raiding spot, but other people are depending on you to make it to raid. If there is an issue where you are unable to make it to a night of raiding, you must post this information in the #attendance tab of discord, preferably a few hours before raid. This will allow the officers time to find a replacement in the case of your absence

The trialing period will last 2 weeks, during this time you will be given the opportunity to show why you belong in the guild. Do not focus on damage or healing parses, we value mechanics over everything, so failing/ignoring them is the number one reason why trials fail.
